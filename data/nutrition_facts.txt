Bananas are rich in potassium and vitamin B6. They also contain fiber, manganese, and vitamin C.
Avocados are high in monounsaturated fats and are a good source of potassium, magnesium, and folate.
Spinach contains iron, calcium, vitamin A, and is a great source of magnesium.
Sweet potatoes provide fiber, vitamin A (beta-carotene), and potassium.
Apples contain fiber and vitamin C. They also provide various antioxidants.
Eggs are rich in protein, vitamin B12, and choline.
Oats are rich in soluble fiber and can help lower cholesterol levels.
Carrots are high in beta-carotene (vitamin A), fiber, and antioxidants.
